Hot chocolate Recipe

This recipe will create the best hot chocolate you will ever experience. It's thick, creamy, and very rich, so sip slow ;).

Likes: 3

Ingredients & Materials

  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 packs of dark hot chocolate powder
  • 2 tbs condensed milk
  • 2 tsp honey
  • small pinch of fine salt
  • Optional:
  • whip cream
  • marshmellows
  • mint

Instructions

  1. gather all ingredients and materials
  2. warm up milk in pot on stove til steaming
  3. add 2 packs of dark hot chocolate powder
  4. stir till completely incorporated
  5. turn off stove
  6. add rest of ingredients
  7. stir till completely incorporated
  8. pour into favorite mug and add optional toppings
  9. enjoy!
